SOLAR SYSTEM 
                    by Joel Shaji 

        ACTIONS: 
        -> Tap on any planetary body to shift focus onto it.
        -> Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and zoom out to observe the selected 
           planetary body at a closer or farther distance

        SUGGESTIONS: 
        -> Tap the sun to center the view. You an also tap the minimap sun to do the same. 
        -> To switch between planetary bodies other than the sun, I suggest tapping the 
           sun first to get a centered view and then tap the planetary body.
        -> If you cannot see a certain body, zoom out using the scroll wheel until 
           it is visible.
        -> For all the star wars fans, I suggest you give the start menu a moment 
           before you press play. 
        -> Good luck catching Mercury :P
